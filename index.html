Welcome to Hunger Harvest

Our services Donate Food?

Hunger harvest is a intiative taken in order to reduce the food wastage in mass gatherings.The aim of its intiative is to collect food from the gatherings and providing the same for the poor and needy.

Be a part of this intiative by donating the excess food left over in your function so we can provid it to the needy

  • We support the needy by providing the excess food
  • Supplying food for Old Age Homes and Orphanages
  • Be a part of this intiative and bring smiles to needy people

The main objective of this intiative is to maintain an equal balance between the two societies in this life. We always have surplus food in the mass gatherings instead of throwing the food away you can provide it to us . We will quality check the food that has been sent to us and we will be delivering to the near by orphanages and old age home.
